OUR SPECIALITY

At Global Events & Studios, we specialize in capturing every minute detail, custom, and ritual of Brahmin wedding
that adorns celebrating love and tradition.

Virtham

Vratham ceremony marks the groom entering the next stage or ashram of his life, from Brahmacharya to Grihasta.

Nitchyathartham

The Nichayathartham is an engagement ceremony where the bride and the groom exchange rings on a day.

Mugurtham

The father of the bride places his daughter’s hand on the groom’s hand, his son-in-law will take care of her daughter in the future.

Reception

Both the families seek the blessings of these Brahmins who are believed to represent the ancestors of the groom and the bride.

Nalangu

Nalangu happens post lunch, where the bride's family and
the room's family get to know each other through a series of activities.
